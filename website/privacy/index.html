Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Tab Memory is a browser extension that helps you save open tabs.

Data we collect

None. Tab Memory does not collect personal data.

Data stored on your device

All information stays only on your computer:

  • Screenshot of the tab at save time
  • Page URL and title
  • Your note
  • Date and time of save

This data is not synced to our servers or third parties.

Permissions

Permission Why
activeTabCapture a screenshot when you click Save
tabsRead tab title/URL, close tab, open history
<all_urls>Screenshot and reopen saved pages
storageStore screenshots and metadata locally
