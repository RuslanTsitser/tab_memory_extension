FAQ

Does Tab Memory sync across devices?

No. Everything is stored locally in your browser. We intentionally avoid cloud sync to keep your browsing context private.

What data is stored?

Screenshot (JPEG), page URL, page title, your note, and save timestamp. Nothing is sent to any server.

Can I save chrome:// pages?

No. Browser internal pages cannot be captured for security reasons. Regular web pages work fine.

How do I delete everything?

Open History and click “Clear history”, or uninstall the extension. Both remove local data from IndexedDB.

Your data stays on your device

Tab Memory collects nothing. No analytics, no accounts, no third-party sharing. Read the full policy for Chrome Web Store compliance.
